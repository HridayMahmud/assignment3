Junior Program

The goals of junior hockey are to promote, develop, and administer the domestic USA Hockey program for junior-aged players, teams, and leagues. USA Hockey's Junior Program is available to athletes who are at least 16 years of age and no older than 20 as of the 31st day of December of the current season of competition.
